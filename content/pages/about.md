  <h1>About StitchIndex</h1>

  <p>StitchIndex is a hand-curated directory of free sewing resources from across the internet — patterns, guides, tutorials, and preserved archives. It exists because finding genuinely free, high-quality sewing content is harder than it should be.</p>

  <h2>Why this exists</h2>

  <p>The internet has always had an incredible wealth of free sewing knowledge scattered across personal blogs, forums, and small websites. Much of it is buried in search results behind commercial pattern stores, or lost to time as old sites go offline. StitchIndex tries to surface and preserve the best of it.</p>

  <p>That includes the early Make Your Own Gear (MYOG) community — a tradition of hikers and outdoors people who shared free patterns and techniques long before it was fashionable. Resources like Ray Jardine's original tarp and shelter guides, early BackpackingLight.com threads, and backpacking.net archives are all part of this history, and they deserve to be findable.</p>

  <h2>How it works</h2>

  <p>Every listing is manually reviewed before it's added. No automated scraping, no affiliate links, no sponsored placements. If a link appears here, it's because it's genuinely useful and freely available.</p>

  <p>If a link has died, I try to find a working Wayback Machine archive and link to that instead, flagging it clearly so you know what you're getting.</p>

  <h2>Submitting a resource</h2>

  <p>If you know a free pattern, guide, or tutorial that should be here, <a href="/submit/">submit it for review</a>. Community suggestions are how the directory grows.</p>
